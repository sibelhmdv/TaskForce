About Hour Of Code Project

On December 11, 2020, at 15:00 in “Deyanet”, Baku Turkish Lyceum, a virtual "Hour of Code" event was held by team 8. During the event, we showed presentations about ADA, the advantages of Computer Science and motivational videos to encourage students. 23 students participated in that event, and at the end of the project, they received certificates due to their participation.
